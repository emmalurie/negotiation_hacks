Hacking the Negotiation

In Women Don’t Ask: Negotiation and the Gender Divide, authors Linda Babcock and Sara Laschever cite dozens of studies that illustrate that women negotiate 30 percent less often then men. Women often cite lack of self-confidence when asking for a raise.

We created Negotiation Hacks to help women gain the self-confidence and tools to be successful negotiators.
